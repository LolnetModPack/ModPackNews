Kingdoms Updated to v1.1.0! - (Posted on September 23, 2014, by 0zlw)

Work on the Lolnet Kingdoms server is continuing. We are currently waiting on a useable protection mod to ensure player's houses and towns remain protected. This is currently a work in progress but we hope to have something out soon.

Announcing Kingdoms! - (Posted on September 21, 2014, by 0zlw)

Kingdoms main mods are Millenaire, Minecraft Comes Alive and Biomes O' Plenty.
